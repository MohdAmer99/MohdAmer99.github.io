Project 3

Interactive Patient Dashboard

A Power BI dashboard that transforms raw patient data into an interactive command center. Supervisors see the full picture in seconds: who is critical, which ward is at capacity, and where attention is needed most. Every chart, card, and table is cross-filtered, turning static numbers into actionable clinical intelligence.

The Challenge & The Solution

Hospital leaders need real-time situational awareness across wards, but patient data often lives in disconnected spreadsheets, EMR exports, and handover notes that no one has time to read.

Before

  • Patient census scattered across paper logs and Excel sheets
  • No at-a-glance view of critical vs. stable patient distribution
  • Ward capacity status requires manual headcounts
  • Priority trends invisible until a crisis emerges
  • No way to filter data by ward, priority, or ventilator status instantly

After

  • Single dashboard displays every patient across all wards
  • Color-coded KPI cards show priority counts at a glance
  • Ward-level bar chart reveals capacity and acuity distribution
  • Interactive slicers filter the entire view in one click
  • Cross-filtering connects every visual for instant drill-down

Dashboard in Action

From high-level KPIs to granular patient details, every insight is one click away.

Full Dashboard View
Command Center
The complete command center in a single screen. Six priority-coded KPI cards anchor the top row, a donut chart breaks down the patient priority mix, and a stacked bar chart reveals ward-level acuity at a glance. Below, a scrollable patient census table exposes every clinical detail from diagnosis to length of stay, while four slicers on the right let supervisors slice the entire view by ward, priority, ventilator dependency, or payment type.
Charts & Census Table
Analytics Layer
The analytical core of the dashboard. The donut chart quantifies the priority distribution across all 30 patients, while the stacked bar chart maps patient volume per ward, color-coded by acuity level. The census table below provides row-level detail including MRN, diagnosis, infection status, and length of stay, with priority-based conditional formatting that lets critical cases stand out instantly.
Interactive Slicer Panel
Filter Engine
Four purpose-built slicers give supervisors surgical control over the data. Filter by ventilator dependency to isolate mechanically ventilated patients, narrow by ward to assess unit-level workload, select a priority tier to focus on critical cases, or segment by payment type for administrative reporting. Every selection propagates across all visuals in real time.

Built on a 30-Patient Sample

This dashboard was developed using a curated sample of 30 simulated patients across three wards (ICU, Ward A, Ward B) to demonstrate the full range of analytical capabilities. The underlying architecture is designed to scale seamlessly and can be tailored to accommodate any institution's departmental structure, patient volume, acuity classifications, and compliance requirements.
