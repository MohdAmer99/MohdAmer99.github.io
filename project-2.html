Medical Equipment Tracking System

A scalable web platform that delivers real-time visibility into every piece of medical equipment across hospital departments — from location tracking and malfunction escalation to automated maintenance scheduling — designed to adapt to any institution's workflows and compliance requirements.

The Challenge & The Solution

Hospitals operate with thousands of medical devices spread across multiple floors and departments. Knowing where a device is, whether it works, and when it needs maintenance should not require a phone call.

Before

  • No centralized system to track equipment location
  • Malfunctioning devices go unreported for days
  • Manual paper logs for maintenance schedules
  • Equipment lost between departments with no accountability
  • No way to search by device name or serial number

After

  • Real-time equipment tracking across all departments
  • Instant malfunction reporting and escalation workflow
  • Automated maintenance scheduling and reminders
  • Full audit trail for every device movement and transfer
  • Powerful search by name or serial across all sections

Key Capabilities

Built from the ground up to address the real pain points of hospital equipment management.

Smart Search

Find any device instantly by name or serial number. Search spans across all sections: active, malfunction, in repair, store, and room assignments.

Device Registration

Add new equipment to the inventory in seconds. Each device is tracked by name and serial number from the moment it enters the system.

Malfunction Reporting

Flag malfunctioning devices directly from the interface. Reports trigger workflows that ensure timely repair and prevent equipment downtime.

System in Action

A look inside the platform that brings order to hospital equipment management.

Universal Device Search
Search Module
Toggle between name-based and serial-based search. Queries span all sections simultaneously, including active devices, those in repair, malfunction reports, store inventory, and room assignments.
Store Inventory
Registration Module
Streamlined device registration form. New equipment is added to the store inventory with device name and serial number, making it immediately searchable and trackable across the system.
Total Devices Overview
Export Module
A complete inventory snapshot at your fingertips. Supervisors can view every registered device across all departments and export the full dataset as CSV or PDF in one click, complete with timestamps and audit trail for compliance reporting.
System Landing Page
Mobile View
The system entry point provides role-based access. Supervisors log in to manage inventory, while frontline staff can immediately report equipment issues without authentication barriers.
Malfunction Report Form
Mobile View
Guided reporting workflow: staff select the report category and device type from a pre-populated list of all registered equipment. Structured input ensures accurate, actionable reports every time.
